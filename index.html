What is Kasai Ruby Studios?

Kasai Ruby Studios is an Indie Video Game Development Company That uses mostly Two-Dimensional graphics. It started as an YouTube Animation Channel in the year 2020, then it grew to 50 subs and I decided to work on my dream carrer being a video game designer.

Where do the games take place?

The games are based in a modern-fantasy world in an made up asain country that is highly advanced compared to most other Countries. If you want to learn more go to Kasai Ruby Wiki for more information.
