Information About Me

Until about 18 months ago I got my an animation program called Blender on my computer. After about a month and a half I made a YouTube channel Called Kasai Ruby and posted an animation video. For about the next seven months I was just make tutotials on animation, speed paints and random videos that I wish I can take down Until august 7th I made my first animated skit. I pushed it of to the side due to the fact it was a big project and I didn't have a lot of time due to school. I Didn't work on this project until early may and thought to post it on a Website dedicated to games that I created. After I release Elite Elemental Brawlers i have plans for two other games afterwards..
