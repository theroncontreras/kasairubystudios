First Ever Kasai Ruby Studios Video Game in Developement!

June 1, 2021 by Mr. Ruby

In about October I had an idea to create a fighting style game featuring Akairio and eleven other characters. So in Early-mid may, i decided to actually work on the idea because i have no school an plenty of time to get a good amout of it done.
